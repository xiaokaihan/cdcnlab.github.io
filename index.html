认知发展机制

How do children develop cognitive abilities like attention, memory, and executive functions? What are the neural mechanisms underlying these developments?

How does the brain change and adapt during child development? What factors influence neural plasticity in early life?

How can we apply neuroscience findings to improve learning and education? What are the optimal conditions for cognitive development?
